# Pentatonic Waltz

**Tradition/source:** Original vessel-flute study
**Public domain:** Yes - pre-1929 US or original MIT
**Arrangement:** Starter L2 arrangement for `vessel-flutes`.

## Range check
C5-A5: within the beginner starter range for this packet.

## Practice tip
Let the first beat settle before moving through the two lighter beats.

## Known gaps
