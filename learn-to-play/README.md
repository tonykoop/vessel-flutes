# learn-to-play / Vessel Flutes (C)

Starter songbook for C vessel flutes and ocarina-like prototypes from the build packet. Generated by the sheet-music skill.

Vessel flutes reward clean breath pressure and stable hand coverage. These tunes avoid large leaps while the tuning experiments settle.

Range: **C5 - A6 practical beginner range, depending on chamber volume and hole layout.**

## Notes on notation
Fingerings are prototype defaults. Re-tune the chart after final chamber volume and hole undercut are validated.

## L2 readiness and known gaps
This starter songbook is ready for L2 review as a playable packet draft: ABC melodies, per-tune notes, static fingering summaries, warmups, and a top-level chart are present. Known gaps: generated audio, MusicXML, LilyPond, MIDI, and printable songsheets are skipped until the shared render dependencies are available; fingering charts need builder review against the final hole layout.
